Output: Research Pack

Generate scripts, narration, hooks, dialogue, lesson copy, CTAs, versions, and approvals.

Output: Approved Script

Convert script into narrative, curriculum, objectives, sequence, examples, checks, and structure.

Plan scenes, shots, camera, motion, transitions, demonstrations, text, and graphics.

Output: Storyboard
06 Visual Studio

Generate images, characters, diagrams, charts, maps, thumbnails, brand visuals, and assets.

Output: Visual Asset Pack
07 Video Studio

Create scene videos, animation, presenter footage, motion graphics, effects, and render previews.

Output: Video Scenes
08 Audio Studio

Create narration, dialogue, music, effects, ambience, cleanup, mixing, and loudness output.

Output: Mixed Audio
09 Timeline Studio

Synchronize tracks, subtitles, graphics, transitions, render preview, and master timeline.

Output: Complete Timeline
10 Quality & Compliance

Review content, script, facts, accessibility, copyright, brand, platform rules, and safety.

Output: Final Approval

Export MP4, CapCut package, subtitles, audio, scripts, storyboard, social and publishing package.
